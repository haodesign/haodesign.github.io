ABOUT ME

Friendly, collaborative, and always learning.

This is Hao, currently a senior student at Taipei, Taiwan. I have a strong interest in UI/UX design as well as grasping the technicalities that make it happen. I enjoy solving complex problems by using a holistic design process Beliving great product starts with understanding how people think.

As a designer, Not only pursue the aesthetics of the visual level, but also the core user needs. I hope that through the power of design, I will create products that are close to people's hearts, enrich the lives of more people, and enjoy the fun of design, with full of enthusiasm and progress towards ideal design.
